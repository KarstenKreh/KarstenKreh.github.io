How can an original and unique web presence succeed in a very traditional sector?

---

About the company

FiNUM.Klinik Makler advises clinical institutions and independent doctors on insurance-related issues in germany. As a brand of FiNUM.Finanzhaus AG it belongs to JDC Group AG, the largest and leading broker pool in Europe. Therefore, the insurance broker can provide technical experts and a wide range of products even in special markets.

The challange

The brand was founded to give special importance to the consulting of health care facilities. Therefore the brand also needed a digital representation.

Preconditions

The design guidelines of FiNUM.Finanzhaus AG provided an excellent basis for the design of the project. With a mixture of high-class and sophisticated but also abstract and expressive design, the company positioned itself in a very individual and outstanding way.

Approach

The use of bright colors on a dark background, an abstract visual language in combination with module frames was adopted for the UI design of the website.
